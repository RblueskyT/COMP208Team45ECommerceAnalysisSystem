Using Instructions

① How to log in?
a. Firstly, please move your mouse pointer on "System Login" at the top navigation bar and then click to choose your identity (Sales Manager, Database Administrator, Safety Specialist and Data Analyst).
b. Secondly, enter your id and password to authenticate your identity. If you forget your password, please click the link "Forget Your Password?"
② How to get more information about this system?
a. If you have any further questions about using this system, please move to "FAQ" which is under "Contact Us" to find any possible solutions. However, if you still have some issues or suggestions, you can send us an email. You can get our email address in "Contact Information".
b. To get basic introduction of this system, please click "System Introduction" to find more details.
